# LinkedIn Post: Introducing MediLens 🏥🚀

**Headline: Revolutionizing Global Medicine Intelligence with AI 🌍✨**

I'm excited to share what I've been building: **MediLens**, an AI-powered intelligence platform designed to bridge the gap in global pharmaceutical access.

Managing medicine registration, pricing, and shortage risks across 30+ countries shouldn't be a manual struggle. MediLens automates the ingestion of regulatory data from the FDA, EMA, and WHO to provide real-time insights for healthcare professionals, researchers, and advocacy groups.

**What MediLens offers:**
✅ **Registration Lag Analysis:** Identify exactly how long it takes for life-saving drugs to reach different markets.
✅ **AI-Driven Risk Profiles:** Instant, deep-dive analysis of supply chain vulnerabilities and pricing barriers.
✅ **Global Search & Tracking:** Monitor the approval status and availability of generic and brand-name molecules worldwide.
✅ **Transparent Data Quality:** Every record is tagged with source metadata and confidence scores for total reliability.

This is just the beginning. I'm building this to make global health data more accessible and actionable.
